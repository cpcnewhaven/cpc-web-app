Getting Connected

We're excited that you're interested in joining our community! Here's how you can get started:

  1. Visit us on Sunday - Join us for worship at 10:30am
  2. Stay for fellowship - Meet people at our fellowship lunch after service
  3. Join a small group - Connect with others in a more intimate setting
  4. Get involved - Find a service opportunity that matches your gifts
  5. Consider membership - Learn about becoming a member of CPC
